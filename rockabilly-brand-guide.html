Four-font system. All loaded from Google Fonts.

font-display / Bebas Neue

BOLD BREW

SECTION HEADERS

Usage: hero headlines, section labels, product name overlays. All-caps only. Letter-spacing: 0.06–0.12em.

font-heading / Boogaloo

Cloud 9 Espresso

Chop Top Dark Roast

Usage: product names, card titles, nav items. Feels hand-lettered without being illegible.

font-serif / Playfair Display

"This ain't your grandpa's coffee."

A harmonious rebellion of beans from three continents.

Usage: pull quotes, testimonials, hero subheads, editorial copy. Use italic sparingly for contrast.

font-body / Barlow

Vibrant berry notes and a zesty citrus kick, followed by a velvety smooth finish that lingers like a sweet dream. Break free from the mundane and ascend to Cloud 9. Order now and experience the difference.

Usage: all body copy, descriptions, UI labels. Weight 400–700. Pairs cleanly with all three display styles.

SPACING & RADIUS

Design decisions for consistent feel across the UI.

Border Radius Convention

rounded-sm — buttons, inputs, cards (primary)
rounded-md — modals, large panels
rounded-full — flavor tags, avatars only

Avoid rounded-lg and rounded-xl — too soft for this aesthetic.
