CHOOSE YOUR MODE

A quick reference for presenting both options to the client.

Dark — Hot Rod Black

  • ✓ Dramatic, night-scene energy
  • ✓ Red neon CTAs stand out strongly
  • ✓ Roastery / craft feel
  • → Better for evening browsing, premium feel
  • → Pinstripe + checker texture as decoration
  • ⚠ Text contrast requires care on dark surfaces

Light — 50s Diner

  • ✓ High energy, bold, immediately readable
  • ✓ Pink CTAs feel fresh, not expected
  • ✓ Checker as structural element, not just trim
  • → Better for daytime browsing, impulse purchase
  • → Ink drop shadows reinforce printed-label craft
  • ⚠ Pink needs restraint — one CTA per section
