Common Questions

Is this a template?

No. Every Packstring site is custom-built — same process we use for every Firefly client. Your site won't look like anyone else's.

Do I need to know anything technical?

No. We build it, we host it, we maintain it. You send us photos and trip info — by text, email, or a quick phone call — and we handle the rest. That's what the $35/mo covers.

Can I update the site myself?

The availability calendar has an admin panel you can use from your phone. For everything else — trip descriptions, photos, pricing changes — just tell us and we'll update it. Usually same-day.

What about my current site?

We'll migrate any content worth keeping — photos, testimonials, trip descriptions, gear lists. If you have a domain you want to keep, we'll handle the DNS transfer.

Do you work with outfitters outside Montana?

Packstring is designed for Montana outfitters, but the approach works for any guide service. If you're out of state and interested, reach out — we'll talk.

What if I already use FishingBooker / The Flybook / FareHarbor?

You can keep using those alongside your Packstring site. But most guides find that once they have a real website with an inquiry form and availability calendar, they don't need the marketplace anymore — and they stop paying the commissions.
