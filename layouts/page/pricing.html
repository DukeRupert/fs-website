Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between a revamp and a new site?

A Site Revamp takes your existing website (Wix, Squarespace, WordPress, etc.) and rebuilds it from scratch with better performance and design. A Brochure Site is built from the ground up for businesses that don't yet have a website. Both include hosting and unlimited edits at the same monthly rate.

What is included in the monthly price?

The monthly price includes hosting, SSL certificate, unlimited content edits, customer support, and ongoing maintenance. Everything you need to keep your site running smoothly is covered.

How long does it take to build my website?

Most websites are completed within 2–4 weeks after we receive your content and sign-off on the design direction. Complex e-commerce projects may take 4–8 weeks.

Do you need my existing content, or do you write it?

We work with content you provide. If you need help writing or editing copy, we can assist — just let us know when you reach out. Photos can be provided by you or sourced from professional stock libraries.
