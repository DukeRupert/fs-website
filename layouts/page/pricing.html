Common Questions

What's the difference between a revamp and a new site?

A Site Revamp takes your existing website — Wix, Squarespace, WordPress, whatever you're on — and rebuilds it from scratch with better performance and design. A Brochure Site is built from the ground up for businesses that don't have a website yet. Both include hosting and unlimited edits at the same monthly rate.

What is included in the monthly price?

Hosting, SSL certificate, unlimited content edits, direct support, and ongoing maintenance. Everything you need to keep your site running. No hidden fees.

How long does it take to build my website?

Most websites are done within 2–4 weeks after we receive your content and sign off on the design direction. Larger projects with custom software may take longer — we'll give you a clear timeline upfront.

Do you need my existing content, or do you write it?

We work with content you provide. If you need help writing or editing copy, we can help — just let us know when you reach out. Photos can be yours or sourced from professional stock libraries.

Not sure which tier fits?

Tell us about your business and we'll point you in the right direction. No pressure, no sales pitch.
