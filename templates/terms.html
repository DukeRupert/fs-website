2. Payment terms

Monthly payment structure

The first two monthly payments are due at proposal signing and constitute the project deposit. These cover discovery, research, and initial design work. The remaining ten payments begin on the date the site launches and are billed monthly on that anniversary.

Upfront payment structure

The full project fee is due at proposal signing. The maintenance rate ($95/month Standard, $150/month Advanced) begins on the date the site launches.

Maintenance rate

After the 12-month build period (or immediately after launch for upfront payments), the maintenance rate ($95/month Standard, $150/month Advanced) covers hosting, SSL certificate management, security updates, and the monthly change hours included in your plan. The maintenance rate may be adjusted with 60 days written notice.

Late payments

Payments more than 15 days overdue may result in temporary suspension of hosting and maintenance services. We will provide written notice before any suspension. Access is restored upon payment of the outstanding balance.

3. Scope and revisions

What's included

Each plan includes a defined number of revision rounds before launch and monthly change hours after launch. A "revision round" means a single set of collected feedback applied across the site. Individual change requests after launch are tracked against the monthly hour allotment.

What counts as a monthly change

Copy updates, new photos, team member additions, updated hours or pricing, adding a page section, and similar content modifications. Major redesigns, new page builds, or new custom functionality are scoped and priced separately outside the monthly allotment.

Unused hours

Monthly change hours do not roll over to the following month.

Scope changes

If the project scope grows beyond what is defined in the signed proposal, Firefly will notify the Client in writing before performing additional work. Additional scope is quoted separately and requires written approval before work begins.
