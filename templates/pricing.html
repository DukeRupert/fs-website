Common questions

Things people ask before signing.

What happens if I want to cancel before month 12?
You can cancel with 30 days notice at any time. Payments made to date are non-refundable — they cover work already completed. You keep the site and can request a file transfer for the standard $150 fee.
Do I own the site?
You own all content and the design from day one. During the agreement, the site lives on Firefly's infrastructure because that's what the hosting covers. After payoff, a full file transfer is $150.
What counts as a monthly change?
Copy updates, new photos, team member changes, updated hours or pricing, adding a page section. Major redesigns or new page builds are scoped separately. Most requests take well under an hour.
How long does the build take?
Standard sites typically launch in 4–6 weeks. Advanced sites run 6–10 weeks. The timeline depends heavily on how quickly you can provide assets and approvals. We've never missed a deadline we set.
Can I pay upfront and skip the monthly structure?
Yes — toggle to “Pay upfront” above. You save 10% on the build and move straight to the $95/mo maintenance rate after launch. Same site, same work, no financing cost.
What if I need more than 12 pages?
That moves into custom project territory and gets scoped individually. Write and tell us what you're building — most of the time it's more manageable than it sounds.
