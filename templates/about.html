About Firefly

Small by design.
Accountable by nature.

Firefly Software is a small, focused team building custom websites and software for small businesses. We're based in Helena, Montana. We work with clients across the country.

Small isn't a limitation. It's how we stay accountable. Every project has a named owner. Every client has a direct line to the person doing the work. That's not a selling point — it's just how we're built.

The team

The people who do the work

Logan Williams
Co-Founder

Grew up overseas — a missionary kid in Papua New Guinea and the Canadian prairies. Spent years as an air traffic controller in the Air Force, then civilian ATC in Afghanistan and the FAA. Earned a BS in Computer Science while working full-time. Founded Firefly in 2019.

The two careers have more in common than you'd think — both demand precision, clear communication, and building systems that don't fail when it matters.

Where we're from

Built in Montana.
Built for America.

Helena is home. We're proud of that — proud to be part of Montana's economy, proud to build things that help American small businesses compete. But the work doesn't stop at the state line. A business in Florida or California deserves the same accountability that our Helena neighbors get. That's the standard we hold ourselves to regardless of the zip code.

What we won't do

  • Take on a project we can't deliver
  • Quote a price and change it mid-build without a conversation
  • Hand your project to someone you've never met
  • Disappear after launch and let you figure out the rest

We also won't maintain sites we didn't build — if it's on WordPress or Wix and you want someone to manage it, we're not the right fit.

If you want to replace it with something better, that's a different conversation and we're happy to have it.
