The Crew

Guides Who Live on the Water

Seven licensed guides. Most of them fish this river on their days off. You'll get someone who knows where the fish are, not where they were last season.

Owner · Head Guide

Matt Mohar

Born and raised in Helena, Montana. 20+ years fly fishing the Missouri River, close to ten guiding on it. This is home water — not a destination he flies into for the season. He built Lo Mo around guides who feel the same way.

Ria French

Guide

Born and raised in Great Falls. Former paramedic turned full-time guide. On the Trout Unlimited board for the Missouri River Flyfishers chapter.

Colter Day

Guide

Lifetime Montana local. Missouri River and Bitterroot specialist. Patient with beginners, pushes experienced anglers to fish better water.

Rain Keating

Guide

Guided the Snake, Flathead, and Green rivers before the Missouri got its hooks in him. He calls it home water now.

Dylan Huseby

Guide
