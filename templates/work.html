WEBSITES

Nautilus Group Cleaning Services

Commercial cleaning · Kennewick, WA

Nautilus had no web presence — no site, no listings, no digital footprint. We built from scratch: custom site, brand direction, SEO setup, and Google Business Profile. Within four weeks of launch they had received a commercial contract and five new inquiries. They had nothing before. Now they have a pipeline.

A-Team Asphalt

Asphalt and paving · Sumner, WA

A-Team needed a site that matched the scale of their operation — a Pacific Northwest paving company with decades of experience and no digital presence to show for it. We built a full custom site with brand identity, copy, SEO strategy, Google Business Profile, and email infrastructure. Built to rank, built to convert.

A-Team Gutters

Gutter installation · Bonney Lake, WA

A-Team Gutters launched in 2024 with strong trade credentials and no web presence. We built the brand from the ground up — identity, copy, and a custom site designed to generate residential leads in a competitive Pacific Northwest market.

SOFTWARE

Estimating Tool — Western Skies Contracting

Construction · Hamilton, MT

Western Skies was running estimates out of spreadsheets — workable, but slow and error-prone. We built a custom estimating and quoting tool tailored to their specific workflow: line-item pricing, automated updates from local manufacturer price lists, e-signature, and version history. What used to take an afternoon now takes minutes. Spreadsheets to software — that's the whole story.

E-Commerce Platform — Rockabilly Roasting

Coffee roaster · Kennewick, WA

Rockabilly needed more than a storefront. They sell retail direct to consumers and wholesale to cafes and restaurants — two different buying experiences, two different pricing structures, one platform. We built a custom e-commerce solution handling B2C sales, B2B wholesale accounts, and subscriptions. No Shopify, no plugin stack, no monthly ransom to a platform they don't own.

We're selective about the projects we take on. That means the list stays short and the work stays strong. If you want to see something specific — a particular industry, a particular kind of problem — get in touch and we'll tell you honestly what we've built that's closest to it.
